Alive


I want to be alive, truly alive,
Not just the kind of life, that I breathe to survive
A life of happiness and thrive
Where I can be everything that I decide

I want to have fun,
Ignorant of my burdens
In my heart, it's what I truly yearn

I want to be surrounded by a love,
And let it lifts me up when times are dire.
To cultivate a relationship that lasts a lifetime,
With the kind of person who quenches all my desires
And willing to be a beacon of sunshine

I yearn to feel the pleasures of life,
To treasure my existence and escape strife.
To experience the moments that spark my fire,
And elevate me to a state of transcendental desire.

I crave the sensations that leave me breathless,
The ones that make my heart race and mind restless.
To feel alive in a way that's pure and true,
And let my soul soar to vistas anew.

I want it all, every pleasure and delight,
To indulge in every whim and every appetite.
No constraints or limits, no rules or chains,
Just living for myself and my own gains.

I want to immerse myself in the beauty of the world,
And revel in the wonders that are unfurled.
To be present in the now and embrace the flow,
And let my spirit be free to glow.

For life is more than just existing and surviving,
It's about embracing the joys and thriving.
And so, I choose to live each day with intent,
Aching to savor every moment that life presents.

I want to be truly alive
To feel the pulse of life within my veins,
To know that every breath I take is mine,
And revel in the joy that it sustains.

By Zwe Nyi Naing
